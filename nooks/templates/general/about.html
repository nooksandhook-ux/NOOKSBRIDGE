About Nooks

Nooks is a gamified platform designed to empower Nigerian readers by combining reading tracking with productivity tools and rewards.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant community of readers and doers who transform their lives through knowledge and action, supported by a sustainable, rewarding platform.
