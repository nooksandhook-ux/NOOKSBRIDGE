Why Nooks?

Nooks is designed for Nigerian readers, combining reading tracking and productivity tools with a unique reward system. It helps you grow personally while earning ₦10 per verified book quote to cover small expenses like airtime or data.

With Nooks, you can track your reading progress, set goals, and earn points and badges for every book you read. Our gamified system keeps you motivated, while detailed analytics help you build consistent reading habits.

Hook section offers a customizable Pomodoro timer with vibrant themes to keep you focused. Track tasks, analyze productivity patterns, and earn rewards for completed sessions, making work more engaging and effective.

Our quote-based reward system lets you earn ₦10 for every verified non-fiction book quote, helping you afford small daily needs. Plus, earn points and badges for reading and productivity to unlock exciting achievements!

Tailored for Nigerian users, Nooks supports your personal growth with tools to track reading and productivity, while offering financial rewards that resonate with local needs, like airtime or snacks, all in a mobile-friendly, offline-capable app.
